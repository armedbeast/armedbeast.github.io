Coconut Chutney Go back!


Ingredients:
  • Coconut - Grated / Shredded / Cut pieces
    		•  1/2 cup
  • Roasted channa (Bugda dal)
    		•  1/2 cup
  • Coriander
    		•  1/3 cup
  • Green chilli (can use less if don't want spicy)
    		•  5-6 counts
  • Curry leaves
    		•  8-10 leaves
  • Asaphotida (Hing)
    		•  A big pinch
  • Salt
    		•  As per taste
  • Cooking Oil
    		•  1 tbspn
  • Water
    		•  2/3 cup
Method:
Step 1- Cooking raw Ingredients
Heat a saucepan / kadai. Keep the flame to a low.

Step 2- Add Ingredients (preferably in this order)
Oil
Asaphotida
Roasted channa
Green chilli
(heat these for few minutes on low flame)
Curry leaves
Coriander
Coconut

Step 3- Cook on low flame
Keep stirring for few minutes
Notes: Don't let the coconut burn (it should not turn dark brown)
Turn off flame when you see nice golden color on channa
Take out all Ingredients in a separate cup (big enough to hold everything after grinding)
Cool them down

Step 4- Grind to thick paste
Use mixer grinder at low setting
Pour all Ingredients in jar alongwith salt
Run the grinder for around 6-7 seconds
Do this till you get your needed consistency
  • Open lid and mix things with spoon
  • Pour some water
  • Grind again for around 7-8 sconds
